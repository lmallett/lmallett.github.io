Drawing & Animation

I've always loved to draw. I began truly developing my skills starting at around 10 years old. Aside from a single basic design course in high school, I'm entirely self-taught. I primarily consider myself a digital artist, specializing in character art and comic layout. I'm familiar with several professional art tools, including Clip Studio Paint, Paint Tool SAI, Toon Boom Harmony, and Medibang.

At the moment, I'm taking somewhat of a break from drawing and animating to focus on my work in physics. However, there are several projects I look forward to resuming; most important to me is a comic series I am planning.
